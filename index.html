Why Web Development?

My interest in tech began when I was hired to teach robotics — a subject I knew nothing about — to elementary students. I learned basic block programming alongside my students and discovered that I had a knack for troubleshooting bugs. Just a few months later, I met my (future) husband, Grant. He was (and still is) a software engineer and I was intrigued by his work.

Fast forward 5 years and I was ready for a different career. I quit my job and did some soul searching. I wanted a career that would better fit my introverted personality. I love solving problems, finding efficient processes, designing the details, and learning new concepts. I realized that a great career had found me — that's how I became a web developer.
