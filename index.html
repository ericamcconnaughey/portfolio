About Me

Welcome to my portfolio!

I am currently learning web development via online courses and the NuCamp Full Stack Bootcamp. I'm excited to continue learning and developing my tech skills. In my former career as a teacher, I realized I was ready for a different challenge. I quit my job and did some soul searching. That led me to web development!

I am an introvert and I want a career where I can utilize my strengths without draining all of my energy. (Hence why teaching didn't work out for me.) I love solving problems, finding efficient processes, designing the details, and learning new concepts. I also love that tech is a quickly-growing field, many tech roles have flexible schedules, and there are a number of remote job opportunities in web development.
